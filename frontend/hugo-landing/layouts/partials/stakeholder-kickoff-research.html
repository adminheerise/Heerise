Hey ! So you’ve read the project brief and got your gap analysis done. You know what you know, and more importantly, you know what you don’t.

Now it’s time to do some prep work. I want you to spend time researching the project before you reach out to stakeholders.

You have access to a research workspace. Inside, you’ll find four areas to explore: the product and business context, the audience, the stakeholders, and the constraints.

For each area, you can ask questions from a list of suggested ones. The knowledge base will respond. Your job is to ask questions, read responses, think, and save the findings that matter.

You don’t have to explore everything. But you need to get the information you need to form hypotheses of the project and ask questions in the kickoff call.
