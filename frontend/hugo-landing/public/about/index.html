Who Are We?

HeeRise is the pioneering career preparation platform dedicated to education and social science students—fields that are often overlooked by traditional career services. We provide real-life case-based learning, career exploration, and direct employer connections, helping students land meaningful internships and future jobs. For international students, we offer tailored guidance that bridges cultural and market gaps, making HeeRise uniquely positioned to unlock a large, underserved talent pool.

MISSION

To democratize career success by equipping individuals from all backgrounds with the clarity, capabilities, and confidence to thrive in evolving and competitive job markets.

VISION

To provide an AI-powered career development platform that guides individuals through three essential phases of career success:

  • Career Discovery & Market Alignment - Help users identify their optimal career paths by analyzing their strengths, interests, and market demand
  • Targeted Skill Development - Deliver personalized learning experiences to build in-demand skills and bridge capability gaps
  • Strategic Job Search & Career Coaching - Provide expert guidance on job hunting strategies, application optimization, and interview preparation
